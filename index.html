Healthcare Workforce Intelligence & Strategy Dashboard

Project Overview

Delivering real-time, actionable insights into healthcare workforce dynamics, empowering smarter hiring strategies, career planning, and business outcomes.

Audience: Recruiters, HR consultants, healthcare job seekers.

Data Source: IBM HR Analytics Dataset

Attrition Trends

  • Higher attrition among mid-level roles and lower-education employees.
  • Female attrition rate exceeds male attrition across divisions.
  • Sales and HR divisions face the highest turnover risks.

Job Engagement Insights

  • Work-life balance declines with age, especially post-40.
  • R&D and Medical Research departments show highest engagement scores.
  • Mixed satisfaction levels among entry-level roles.

Role Demand

  • Clinical certifications remain in high demand across healthcare roles.
  • Growing shortages in IT support roles in healthcare technology.
  • Cross-functional skills attract premium salaries and faster placements.

Revenue Insights

  • Patient care roles drive highest revenue contribution.
  • Higher career readiness correlates with higher income potential.
  • Top three revenue-generating fields are Clinical Care, R&D, and Specialized Consulting.
