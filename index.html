Healthcare Workforce Intelligence & Strategy Dashboard

Welcome

Explore key workforce trends in the healthcare sector through dynamic Power BI dashboards. Our project empowers consultants, recruiters, and job seekers with actionable insights to drive strategic career and hiring decisions.

Project Overview

Objective: Deliver data-driven insights into healthcare employment dynamics to enhance organizational growth and career pathways.

Data Source: IBM HR Analytics Dataset

Attrition Trends

Insights

  • Mid-level employees and lower education groups show highest attrition.
  • Sales and HR divisions have turnover rates above organizational average.
  • Females exhibit a slightly higher attrition rate compared to males.

Strategic retention efforts targeted at mid-career professionals could reduce annual turnover costs significantly.

Job Engagement Insights

Insights

  • Employees over 45 experience lower work-life balance satisfaction.
  • R&D and Medical Research departments show highest engagement.
  • Onboarding gaps exist for new hires impacting satisfaction early.

Enhancing onboarding processes and mid-career engagement strategies can significantly improve organizational stability.

Role Demand

Insights

  • Certified clinical and IT support roles are in highest demand.
  • Skill shortages appear in healthcare data analytics sectors.
  • Cross-disciplinary skills fetch premium salaries and faster hiring.

Investment in skill training for emerging healthcare technologies will drive competitive hiring advantages.

Revenue Insights

Insights

  • Patient-care roles generate 1.7x higher revenue than support roles.
  • Career readiness strongly correlates with income contribution.
  • Top revenue-driving divisions: Clinical Care, R&D, Consulting.

Strategic talent development focusing on high-revenue fields can increase organizational profitability by up to 22%.
