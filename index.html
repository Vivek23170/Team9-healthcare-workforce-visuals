Healthcare Workforce Intelligence & Strategy Dashboard

Project Objective: Deliver real-time, actionable insights into healthcare workforce dynamics, enabling smarter hiring, career planning, and organizational strategies.

Audience: Recruitment consultants, HR professionals, workforce strategists, and healthcare job seekers seeking market insights.

Data Source: IBM HR Analytics Dataset on Kaggle

Attrition Trends

Key Insights:

  • Attrition rates segmented by gender and job role
  • Turnover trends across divisions and departments
  • Correlation of salary distribution with education levels

Job Insights

Key Insights:

  • Employee engagement levels across different departments
  • Work-life balance variations by age group
  • Analysis of role stability and satisfaction metrics

Role Demand

Key Insights:

  • Identification of high-demand skills across healthcare roles
  • Analysis of average daily pay rates by division
  • Career pathways and emerging skillsets

Revenue Insights

Key Insights:

  • Income vs. revenue contribution analysis by job role
  • Career readiness scoring based on financial impact
  • Top three revenue-generating healthcare fields
