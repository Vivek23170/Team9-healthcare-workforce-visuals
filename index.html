Welcome to Our Project

Explore healthcare workforce trends through dynamic Power BI dashboards. Our insights empower recruiters, consultants, and job seekers to make smarter decisions using real-time analytics.

Project Introduction

Objective: Deliver meaningful, data-driven insights into healthcare employment dynamics, enhancing organizational and career strategies.

Target Audience: Recruiters, workforce strategists, healthcare professionals, and career aspirants.

Data Source: IBM HR Analytics Dataset

Attrition Trends

Insights:

  • Higher attrition observed among mid-level roles and employees with lower education levels.
  • Female attrition slightly surpasses male rates across most divisions.
  • Sales and HR departments face the highest turnover risks.

Addressing attrition hotspots through targeted retention strategies can enhance workforce stability and reduce rehiring costs significantly.

Job Engagement Insights

Insights:

  • Work-life balance satisfaction declines slightly with age progression beyond 40 years.
  • Research & Development and Medical departments show the highest employee engagement.
  • Entry-level positions experience varied satisfaction rates, highlighting onboarding improvement opportunities.

Enhancing employee engagement based on department-specific feedback can boost overall organizational performance and reduce voluntary exits.

Role Demand Analysis

Insights:

  • Clinical certifications remain highly demanded across healthcare divisions.
  • Healthcare IT support roles show emerging skill shortages, offering new career opportunities.
  • Cross-functional skills (clinical + technical) command higher pay and faster hiring cycles.

Focusing upskilling efforts towards high-demand domains can maximize employability and align with emerging healthcare trends.

Revenue Insights

Insights:

  • Patient-facing roles drive the highest revenue per employee contributions.
  • Career readiness scores align positively with income growth potential.
  • Top revenue-driving fields include Clinical Care, Research, and Specialized Consulting Services.

Organizations investing in skill development aligned with revenue-driving roles can enhance financial performance while retaining high-potential talent.
