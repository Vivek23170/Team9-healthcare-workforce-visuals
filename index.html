Healthcare Workforce Intelligence & Strategy Dashboard

Welcome to Our Project

Explore key workforce trends in the healthcare sector through dynamic Power BI dashboards. Our analysis empowers recruiters, HR consultants, and job seekers with actionable insights to drive strategic decisions and career growth.

Project Overview

Objective: Deliver clear, data-driven insights into healthcare workforce dynamics to improve hiring, employee engagement, and organizational planning.

Target Audience: Recruitment consultants, HR professionals, healthcare strategists, and job seekers.

Data Source: IBM HR Analytics Dataset

Attrition Trends

  • Mid-level employees and lower education levels show higher attrition rates.
  • Female attrition rates are slightly higher compared to male counterparts.
  • Sales and HR divisions exhibit the highest turnover risk.

Job Engagement Insights

  • Employee satisfaction declines slightly with age progression.
  • Research and Medical departments showcase high engagement and stability.
  • Entry-level onboarding processes present opportunities for improvement.

Role Demand

  • Clinical and certified roles remain highly in-demand across sectors.
  • IT support roles in healthcare face emerging skill shortages.
  • Cross-functional competencies boost career advancement opportunities.

Revenue Insights

  • Patient-facing roles generate the highest per-employee revenue contributions.
  • Career readiness positively correlates with income potential.
  • Top revenue-driving domains include Clinical Care, Research, and Consulting.
