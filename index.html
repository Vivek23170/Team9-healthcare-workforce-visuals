Project Overview

Objective: Provide a comprehensive overview of workforce patterns in the healthcare industry using visual analytics.

Data Source: IBM HR Analytics Dataset

Attrition Trends

Insights

  • Highest attrition occurs in mid-level employees and sales roles.
  • Medical research and healthcare HR divisions are heavily affected.
  • Attrition rate is 60% female vs. 40% male.

Targeted retention programs are needed for clinical research coordinators and sales specialists.

Job Engagement Insights

Insights

  • Work-life balance declines with age, especially beyond 40.
  • Job satisfaction scores are highest in Medical Research divisions.

Enhancing work flexibility policies for senior staff could improve overall engagement.

Role Demand

Insights

  • Clinical Research Coordinators and Health Service Managers are in highest demand.
  • Leadership and decision-making skills show strong correlation with demand level.

Upskilling programs targeting leadership, planning, and empathy would directly support high-demand roles.

Revenue Insights

Insights

  • Pharmaceutical Sales Representatives generate highest annual revenue: $947K/year.
  • Medical Lab Technologists follow with strong average monthly income.

Targeting roles with high ROI while maintaining employee satisfaction can optimize workforce planning.
